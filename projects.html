My trading simulator - in Python

When I started, I literally started with a paper and pen. But quickly reailsed that wasn't for me lol. I can't do that many calculations. I HATED it.

Then I tried Excel and other spreadsheet programs, but I was too lazy to learn how to use them and also, it just didn't "feel" like trading.

Then I tried to find paper trading simulators online. I found a few, but nothing that satisfied me. So I decided to make my own.

I knew enough programming to know it was possible. I didn't know enough to do it. So I asked ChatGPT for help. Spent hours talking to it, trying to get it right. I won't go into details, but it was long and painful lol.

I use TradingView now. I would have used it then if I knew it existed, but hey, no regrets. I learnt a lot of things. I'm glad I got that experience. Also it's a nice flex (which is why I'm mentioning it).

I actually really enjoyed paper-trading with my simulator, because... it was mine! TradingView, though much better in almost every aspect, is not "mine". I still mess with my program sometimes haha.
