The Internet Finance Fraud Epidemic

And Why You’re Falling for It

Let’s be real — online trading “education” is a circus. Especially on YouTube, where every other video hawks some $49.99/month Discord with “real insider knowledge.”

Spoiler: you don’t get any. If you pay, you’re the sucker.

No one posting flashy 30-min trading videos every few days with bulls and bears making out in the thumbnail is trading seriously.

No one in rented Lambos, flexing cash, is trading seriously. No one who has the energy to be a full-time content creator focused on selling you the “secret skills” is trading seriously.

Because Trading Is Not a Vibe

Trading isn’t a flashy side quest. It’s exhausting. It’s stressful. Most people lose money.

Seriously, there’s a saying:
90% of retail traders lose 90% of their money in 90 days.

Your favorite YouTube guru with the Deadpool profile pic promising “market secrets”? Fraud. The guy selling a custom indicator that “automatically trades for you”? Probably fraud.

And if you buy into that, you’re umm... regarded. Highly regarded.

There’s No Shortcut

No strategy always works. No tool guarantees profits. Most indicators just simplify what the chart is already screaming at you.

Want to get better? Learn to sit with the chaos. Accept that the market is… well, a bit of a b!tch — and still control your emotions, think clearly, and don’t freak out. That’s when progress happens.
