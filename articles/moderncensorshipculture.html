I Am Offended. And It's Your Problem.

When Did Words Start Breaking People?

People used to speak freely. Some were dumb. Some were crude. But no one shattered like glass over it. That’s changed. Now words are weapons, and the world is full of people waiting to be hurt.

You don’t even have to mean it. You just have to say it wrong. Use the wrong tone. Forget a rule you didn’t know existed. Then it’s over. One screenshot, one tweet, one thread — and you’re done.

The Mob Doesn't Need Torches

A few people get angry online. Then more join. Brands panic. CEOs release statements. Posts get deleted. Lives get erased. All because someone had a bad morning and needed a new target.

The apologies come fast. Polished. Bloodless. “We’re listening.” “We’re learning.” “We stand with…” They don’t believe it. You don’t believe it. But we all pretend it matters.

What’s the Cost of Being Safe?

You can sell your body and know the price. That’s honest. Selling your soul is quieter. It feels like safety. It feels like progress. But it’s neither. You give a little. Then a little more. Soon, you don’t recognize the sound of your own thoughts.

People speak in corporate dialect now. It's all soft edges and empty meaning. They say the right words to survive. Not to communicate. Not to connect.

The Overcorrection

We used to want kindness. Now we want control. One wrong word? Gone. One old post? Done. It’s not about justice anymore. It’s about theater. A public trial for likes.

We act like hurt feelings are holy. Like being offended gives you moral power. It doesn’t. It gives you attention. That’s all most of this is: performance.

The Soul Shrinks in Silence

Every time we swallow a joke, a thought, a question — we shrink. Our speech gets safer. So do our ideas. And soon, there’s nothing left but consensus and fear. That’s not a society. That’s a prison made of politeness.

Freedom isn’t supposed to feel this polite. Real talk is messy. Real minds offend. That’s what makes it real.

Here’s a Thought

Maybe you don’t need an apology. Maybe you need to grow up.

Maybe you don’t need the world to bend around your wounds. Maybe you need to toughen the hell up.

Because the future we’re heading for? Where every word is lawyered, filtered, and flattened? That world will be cold. And fake. And silent.

If you’ve made it this far looking for something to be offended by, go ahead and be.

Fuck you. You earned it.
