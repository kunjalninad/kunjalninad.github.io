About Me!

>Click here for RESUME!

I’m a discretionary equities trader — meaning I trade stocks based on a mix of experience, intuition, and, occasionally, cat meows. (Just kidding. Mostly.)

I believe markets are chaotic and fundamentally unpredictable. No algorithm or system can operate without human judgment. In a world where Cristiano Ronaldo moving a Coke bottle can wipe out millions in market cap, I think it’s fair to say my little Python script can’t predict everything.

That said, I’m not some technophobe waving my cane at AI models. I love learning how computers work and using tech to enhance my trading. I even built my own trading simulator — before I realized TradingView already has one, for free. But for now, I’ll stick to discretion over equations.

Outside of trading, I try not to be a finance bro. I like great movies, good books, long walks, and spending time with friends. I’m not the kind of hustler who hires someone to do everything for him while he's making money. I make my own bed, brush my own teeth, and yes, I kiss my girlfriend myself. I don't want to hire a professional for that.

Oh, and I also cook. Wait - please don't fall in love with me just yet. I’m not 6'2, and I'm not a trust fund baby. I also don't have blue eyes T_T.
But I *do* have a guitar... just saying ;)
