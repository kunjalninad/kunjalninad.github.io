About me.

I’m a discretionary equities trader — meaning I trade stocks based on a mix of experience, intuition, and, occasionally, cat meows. (Just kidding. Mostly.)

I believe markets are chaotic and fundamentally unpredictable. No algorithm or system can fully replace human judgment. In a world where Cristiano Ronaldo moving a Coke bottle can wipe out millions in market cap, it’s safe to say my little Python script won’t see everything coming.

That said, I’m not some technophobe waving a cane at AI models. I love learning how computers work and using tech to enhance my trading. I even built my own trading simulator — before realizing TradingView already has one. For free. But for now, I’ll stick with discretion over equations.

Outside of trading, I’m into great movies, good books, long walks, and spending time with friends. I’m not the kind of hustler who outsources his entire life — hiring someone to make his bed, brush his teeth, and kiss his girlfriend so he can optimize for alpha. I mean, I respect the hustle… but it's not for me, thanks.
